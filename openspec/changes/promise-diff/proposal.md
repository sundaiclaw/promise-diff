## Why
Teams and indie builders routinely overpromise in launch posts, product pages, and changelogs, then struggle to communicate what actually shipped without sounding evasive. The gap between promise and reality creates trust debt, support churn, and awkward release updates.

## What Changes
- Add a comparison flow for "what we promised" versus "what we shipped"
- Extract matches, misses, overclaims, and risky wording with AI
- Draft an honest customer-facing update message
- Present the output in a clear side-by-side web UI

## Capabilities
- New spec: promise-comparison

## Impact
Users can turn launch copy and release notes into a reality-check in minutes, then publish a clearer and more trustworthy update.
